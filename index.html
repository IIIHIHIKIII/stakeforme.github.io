Stake For Me

The ultimate solution to account deadlock issues

What is This?

Stake for Me consists of two smart contracts autostakecpu and autostakenet.

It's designed to help people with deadlocked account issues. And it's completely free to use.

Just grab some EOS from your favourite exchange, and withdraw to the corresponding contract with the memo set to your account name.

Boom! Your account is usable again!

How to Use?

Need CPU Stake?

1. Buy EOS from an exchange

2. Click withdrawal

3. Set the recipient to autostakecpu

4. Set the memo to YOUR OWN ACCOUNT NAME

5. Confirm your withdrawal

Need NET Stake?

1. Buy EOS from an exchange

2. Click withdrawal

3. Set the recipient to autostakenet

4. Set the memo to YOUR OWN ACCOUNT NAME

5. Confirm your withdrawal
